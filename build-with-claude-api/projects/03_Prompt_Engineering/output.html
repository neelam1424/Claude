# Marathon Training Nutrition Plan

## Daily Calorie Target: 4000+ calories
**Macronutrient breakdown:**
- **Carbs: 60-65%** (600-650g) — primary fuel source
- **Protein: 15-17%** (150-170g) — muscle recovery
- **Fat: 20-25%** (90-110g) — hormone function, satiety

---

## Sample Daily Meal Structure

### Breakfast (800 cal)
- 2 cups oatmeal with honey
- 2 bananas
- 2 tbsp almond butter
- 1 cup whole milk

### Mid-Morning Snack (300 cal)
- Sports drink or juice (40-60g carbs)
- Rice cakes with jam
- Handful of dried fruit

### Lunch (1000 cal)
- 200g lean protein (chicken/fish/lean beef)
- 2 cups white rice or pasta
- Vegetables (olive oil for calories)
- Piece of fruit

### Afternoon Snack (400 cal)
- Smoothie: yogurt, oats, berries, honey, banana
- Or: Energy bars + sports drink

### Dinner (1000 cal)
- 180g protein source
- 300g sweet potato/pasta
- Vegetables in olive oil
- Whole grain bread

### Evening Snack (500 cal)
- Cereal with milk, fruit, or bagel with honey

---

## Key Principles
✓ Prioritize easily digestible carbs (white rice, pasta, bread, bananas)  
✓ Time higher carbs around training sessions  
✓ Stay hydrated (3-4L water daily minimum)  
✓ Include electrolytes on long runs  
✓ Keep fiber moderate to avoid GI issues during runs
